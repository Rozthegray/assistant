NinjaTrader: Login Instructions
NinjaTrader Download and Connection Guide
Updated over a week ago

If this is your first time logging in to NinjaTrader, follow these steps:
Go to https://account.ninjatrader.com
Enter your login details, which can be found on your MFFU Dashboard.
Select your preferred trading platform.

NinjaTrader Desktop Setup:
- Launch NinjaTrader Desktop.
- Enter your login credentials (from your MFFU dashboard).
- Click connections on your Dashboard, then Configure.
- Find and select NinjaTrader, then click add.
- Under the Configured window, click My NinjaTrader. In the properties window, enter your Username and Password, set the account type to “Simulation”, click Apply, then OK.
- On your NinjaTrader Dashboard, click Connections again and select My NinjaTrader to connect to your MFFU account.

Connection indicator status:
- Green: Connected
- Yellow: Attempting to connect
- Red: Disconnected from order server
- Transparent: Disconnected completely
