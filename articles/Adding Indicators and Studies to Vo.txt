Adding Indicators and Studies to Volumetrica
Updated over 5 months ago

After configuring layouts/workspaces, you can enhance charts with indicators and studies.

Adding Indicators:
- Click Indicators (top-right).
- Browse or search for indicators (Volume/Technical).
- Select (e.g., MACD), customize, and Apply to chart.
- Save settings as a template for reuse.

Drawing Tools:
- Use pointer tools to navigate charts.
- Drawing tools include: Horizontal/Vertical Lines, Box/Parallel Lines, Text, Fibonacci, Ruler.
- To tweak Fibonacci, double-click lines, adjust settings in popup, save as template.

Support for indicator requests is available with the support team.
