Tips for Choosing Your Plan

 

Picking the right prop firm evaluation plan is key to your trading success. Here’s how to make an informed choice:

Match Your Experience: New to trading? Start with the Starter, Core or Scale Plan’s guided approach. Confident in your skills? Go for the Expert or Pro Plan’s flexibility.

Assess Risk Appetite: The Starter, Core and Scale Plans offers more guidance for cautious traders, while the Expert and Pro Plans suits those comfortable with higher risk.

Consider Costs: Evaluate upfront costs, reset fees, and payout schedules to align with your budget.

Review Rules: Understand profit targets, drawdown limits, and other requirements for each plan to ensure they fit your trading style.

Plan for Growth: Both plans offer resets if you breach rules, keeping your path to funding open.
