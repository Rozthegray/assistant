Workspaces and Templates in Volumetrica
Updated over 5 months ago

Volumetrica supports fully customizable workspaces/layouts:

To create a workspace:
1. Configure charts, symbols, indicators as desired.
2. Save chart as template (Save Template at top-left).
3. At top-right, Save > Add a New Workspace. Name workspace and save for reuse.

To arrange charts, click layout button for multiple configuration options.
To customize appearance, click properties for chart themes/settings, save as template.

For questions, contact support.
