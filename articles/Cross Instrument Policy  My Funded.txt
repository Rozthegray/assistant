Cross Instrument Policy | My Funded Futures Help Center

All traders must follow these rules to ensure fair and compliant trading:

1. Single Instrument Limit: Orders that exceed the allowed contracts on a single instrument (e.g. NQ > 2 contracts) are rejected.
2. Cross Instrument Trading: Combining instruments (e.g. 2 minis + 20 micros) above overall limit may execute, but is NOT permitted!
3. Intentional Rule Bypass: Trying to circumvent scaling rules is a breach (e.g. attempting to scale up by mixing instruments).
