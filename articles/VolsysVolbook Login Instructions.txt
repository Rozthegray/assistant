Volsys/Volbook: Login Instructions
Updated over a week ago

Getting started into Volsys/Volbook is a straightforward process. Simply click the Downloads button from your MFFU dashboard, select Volsys/Volbook, and you will be directed to log in to the dxFeed web platform.
When you click the link, you will be prompted to log in with your platform credentials, which MyFundedFutures will send you via e-mail.

Login Screen:
When you reach the login screen, you will be prompted to enter your credentials. Please enter them carefully, as the password is case-sensitive, and you will gain access to the dxFeed dashboard.

Looking to Get Funded to trade on Volsys/Volbook? Start here.

Once you log in to dxFeed:
- Please select your preferred platform.
- Sign the platform agreements to proceed.
- Go to Download Platform section on your dxFeed dashboard and download the setup for your chosen platform.
- Launch the Volsys/Volbook desktop platform.

In the Startup Wizard, select the Exchange(s) from which you would like to receive market data (CME, COMEX, CBOT, NYMEX).
Select dxFeed as the Feed source and Propfirm as the server. Enter your login credentials from your MFFU dashboard.

Feed Selection:
After completing the setup, the Volsys/Volbook dashboard will launch. To connect your MFFU trading account, ensure you select the data feed to confirm connection to dxFeed. Without selecting a data feed, trading on the platform will not be possible.
Select a connection on the Volsys/Volbook dashboard, choose MFFU, and verify connection (green dot). You are now online and ready to trade via Volsys/Volbook!
