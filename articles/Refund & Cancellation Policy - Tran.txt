Refund & Cancellation Policy - Transparent and Trader-First
Updated over a week ago

At MyFundedFutures, we lead with transparency and fairness. If your plans change, use the guidelines below to request a prop-firm refund or cancel your account.

Refund Policy — Risk-Free if You Haven’t Traded

Eligibility: A full refund is available when no trades have been placed after an account purchase.

How to request: Email [email protected] within 14 days of purchase or connect with us via our live chat on our website.

Processing: We return funds via your original payment method and send a confirmation email once processed.

Crypto payments: For AML compliance, crypto purchases are non-refundable.

Account Cancellation

How to cancel: Email [email protected] with your account details.
After: Access to all MFFU dashboards and services ends immediately. Prior fees are non-refundable.

MFFU’s Right to Terminate

Breaching our General Terms & Conditions can result in account deactivation, forfeiture of funds, and a ban from future MFFU services.

Arbitration & Dispute Resolution

Unresolved disputes go to binding arbitration under American Arbitration Association rules.
By signing up, traders waive chargebacks and court actions, ensuring a fair, streamlined resolution process for everyone.
