GETTING STARTED / GENERAL PLATFORM Q&A

Q: Why can’t I get funded Discord role?
A: Sync Discord in Dashboard → Personal Settings → Update Role.

Q: Connected Discord but no roles?
A: Log out/in again; or contact support.

Q: Can I use debit card?
A: Yes — Visa/Mastercard. No Discover.

Q: How to complete KYC?
A: Dashboard → Personal Settings → KYC. Requires ID + proof of address.

Q: Copy trading multiple accounts shows different contract limits?
A: Scaling updates only after EOD.
