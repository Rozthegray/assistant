How to Trade with TradingView
TradingView Guideline
Updated over a week ago

There are a few options for trading on TradingView.

Long/Short Position Tool Entry (Recommended):
1. Login to Tradovate via TradingView’s Trading Panel.
2. Change to your preferred Futures symbol.
3. Select Long/Short tool for buying or selling.
4. Place on chart, adjust entry, stop & take profit.
5. Right-click to adjust risk and choose ‘Create Limit Order’ (even for market entry).
6. Set entry type (Limit/Market), adjust settings.
7. Press “Buy” or “Sell” to enter.
8. X button cancels/flatten position.

Quick Market Entry:
1. Login as above, select symbol.
2. Adjust quantity, use Buy/Sell for market entry.
3. Manually add stop/take profit after entry: click + near price, select “Stop” or “Limit”.

Flatten/close positions with X.

For discounts, see MFFU Deals page.
