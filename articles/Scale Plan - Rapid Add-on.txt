Live Account Transition Parameters

Upon reaching a cumulative total of $10,000 in simulated profits, your account will be reviewed by the risk team. This milestone allows the team to assess trading performance and determine suitability for transition to a live account.

Note: Cumulative totals include both profits made and payouts. For example:

A trader earns $6,000 in profits and requests $2,000 in payouts. After the payout, the remaining profit is $4,000. However, the cumulative total remains $6,000. The trader then earns another $4,000, reaching the $10,000 milestone with $8,000 in profit.

Once the transition to a live account is initiated, profits up to $5,000 will be deposited into the live account. Any remaining profits are forfeited.
