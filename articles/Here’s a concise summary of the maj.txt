Here’s a concise summary of the **major changes and new plan structure at MyFundedFutures (MFFU) as of July 22, 2025, with further updates on September 10, 2025**:

***

**Universal Changes Across All New Plans**
- **One Standard Evaluation:** Evaluation requirements are now the same for all plans.
- **$0 Activation Fee:** No setup fees on any plan.
- **$100,000 Payout Cap:** The max payout cap per user is raised to $100k from a previous $25k soft cap.
- **No Daily Loss Limit (DLL):** The Daily Loss Limit is eliminated from all new plans.
- **Consistency Rule Changes:** 50% consistency rule applies only in the Evaluation stage across all plans, NOT in the Sim Funded stage.
- **News Trading:** Allowed in Core and Scale, and during the evaluation stage for Pro.
- **Micro Scalping Rule Removed:** Trades over 4 ticks are now accepted.
- **Account Limit Increase:** Up to 5 simulated funded $50k accounts per user (previously 3).
- **Profit Split:** 80/20 for trader/firm on all new plans (legacy plans excluded).

***

**Plan-Specific Details**

- **Core Plan:** (Replaces Starter)
  - Pass evaluation in as fast as 2 days.
  - No DLL, $2,000 funded max loss, $3,000 profit target.
  - $1,000 max payout in funded stage.
  - Legacy Core/Starter being phased out but still available for limited time.
- **Scale Plan:** (Replaces Starter Plus)
  - Tiered payout system: $1,500 cap at first, increases to $3,500 by the fifth payout.
  - No DLL.
  - Higher funded balances for larger accounts.
- **Pro Plan:** (Replaces Expert)
  - Lower profit targets to pass.
  - High max payout cap ($100k/user), with possibility for payouts above cap transitioning to live.
  - High performers may be transitioned to live at any time.
  - More clearly defined transition-to-live rules (3 payouts or every $20k milestone).

***

**Transition to Live Account (Effective September 10, 2025)**
- **Old Option:** “30 profitable days” as a route to live accounts is being discontinued.
- **New Rule:** **To qualify for live on Core or Scale, you must earn five consecutive payouts.**
- For Pro, transition to live can also happen after three consecutive payouts or at every $20k profit milestone.

***

**Additional Notes**
- **No Hidden Costs:** $0 activation fees remain in effect.
- **Trader Flexibility:** Payout caps are much higher; new tiered system is designed for easier scaling.
- **Legacy Accounts:** Can still be purchased for now but will be phased out.
