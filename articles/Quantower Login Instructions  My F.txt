Quantower Download and Connection Guide
Updated over a week ago

Quantower is a versatile and intuitive trading platform that provides both novice and experienced traders with advanced tools for market analysis and seamless execution across various instruments.

Getting Started:
- Download Quantower from your MFFU dashboard.
- Install Quantower.
- Launch Quantower.
- On dashboard, click dxFeed to set up connections. Select MyFundedFutures. Enter your credentials (sent by email from MyFundedFutures), then click Connect.

Trading:
- Account Information: click Acc from dashboard
- Working Orders: click Ord
- Positions: click Pos
- Order Entry: click OE and select instrument from Symbol tab
- Charts: click Cht and search instrument via search bar
