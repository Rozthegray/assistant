Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
Q: How often can I withdraw on the live funded account? 
A:You may request a payout of your profits once every 24 hours once you have reached Live Funded.

 

Q: What is the minimum for requesting? 
A: The minimum for withdrawal is $200 on the Live Funded Account.

 

Q: Can I request a payout on the funds allocated? 
A: The initial funds allocated are to trade, not withdraw. You may earn extra allocation via the Live Bonus program.

 

Q: How does the scaling work?

A: Both plans offer micro-scaling opportunities once you reach the live funded stage, allowing you to gradually increase your trading capacity as you demonstrate consistent profitability.

 

Q: Are there any subscription fees?

A: The evaluation phases of both plans include subscription charges, but these are removed once you reach the live funded stage.

 

Q: What happens if I hit the Daily Loss Limit (DLL) in my live account?

A: Both plans implement a soft breach system for the Daily Loss Limit of $500, allowing for some flexibility in risk management while maintaining account safety.

 

Q: Is T1 trading allowed? 
A: T1 trading is allowed on the 1 Step or 2 Step evaluation phase. 
Bear in mind, trading news on live is allowed, but highly discouraged. 
When trading news live, your trades may not fill as expected, and your Daily Loss may not be respected. MyFundedFutures is not liable for any losses occurred from trading news.
