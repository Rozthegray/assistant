Step-by-Step Payout Process for Sim-Funded Accounts:

Confirm Payout Eligibility: Pass your evaluation and meet the requirements for your sim-funded account. For detailed eligibility criteria, review the Payout Policy Overview.

Complete KYC Verification: Log in to the MFFU dashboard (Personal Settings > KYC) and submit your Know Your Customer (KYC) details.

Set Up a Riseworks Account: Create an account on Riseworks to receive your payouts.

Submit Your Payout Request: Visit the MFFU payout dashboard to submit your request.

Meet Minimum Withdrawal Amounts:

Starter, Core and Scale Accounts: Minimum withdrawal of $250.

Expert & Pro Accounts: Minimum withdrawal of $1,000.

 

What to Expect After Submitting Your Payout Request:

Review Process: Your request is reviewed within 1-2 business days.

First Payout Agreements: If this is your first payout with MyFunded Futures, you’ll receive an email to sign agreements via Riseworks.

Payout Delivery: After signing, your payout is transferred to your Riseworks account. You can withdraw funds via bank transfer or cryptocurrency.

Support Available: If you have questions or need help, contact the MyFunded Futures support team for prompt assistance.
