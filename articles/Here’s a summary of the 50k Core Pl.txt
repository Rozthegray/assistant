Here’s a summary of the **50k Core Plan Flex Add-On** at MyFundedFutures:

***

**What is the Core Flex Add-On?**
- **Introduces an alternative payout structure to the standard 50k Core Plan.**
- **FREE** when you purchase a 50k Core Plan ($77).
- Must be **added at checkout** (cannot be applied after purchase).
- Available for a **limited time**.
- **Evaluation and live transition rules remain unchanged**—only the payout structure is different.

***

**How Flex Payouts Work**
- **Eligibility:** After **5 winning days** (each net PNL ≥ $100), you can request a payout.
- **Payout Amount:** You can request **50% of your net profit per payout, capped at $3,000**.
- **Other 50% remains in your account.**
- **Standard 80/20 Profit Split applies:** You keep 80%, MFFU receives 20%.
- **Minimum payout request:** $250.
- **Maximum Loss Limit:** Once you receive your first payout, the max loss limit locks at $50,100.

**Example Calculations**
- **Example #1:** 5 qualifying days, $4,000 net profit:
  - Eligible payout: $2,000 (50%)
  - After split: Receive $1,600
  - $2,000 stays in account

- **Example #2:** $10,000 net profit:
  - Eligible payout capped at $3,000
  - After split: Receive $2,400
  - $7,000 stays in account

***

**Additional Features**
- **News Trading Allowed:** In both the evaluation and sim funded stages.
- **Scaling Rules:** Contract limits update by account balance (daily):
  - Below $1,000: 1 mini / 10 micro
  - $1,000 – $2,000: 2 minis / 20 micros
  - Above $2,000: 3 minis / 30 micros

***

**Key Points**
- **Flex Add-On ONLY changes the payout and scaling rules, NOT how you qualify or move to live funding.**
- Adds more flexibility for withdrawals while keeping risk controls in place.
- Only available for **a limited time** and must be selected when purchasing.
