Volumetrica: Getting Started
Updated over a week ago

Getting started into Volumetrica web platform is user-friendly:
- Click Login to Volumetrica from dashboard.
- Enter platform credentials (sent by MFFU via email).

Feed Selection:
- After login, select dxFeed to connect.
- To pick the asset, click near timeframes upper-right, enter asset symbol (e.g., NQ, CL).
- Click on the contract to load chart. Adjust timeframe and click Apply.

For connection issues, contact support.
