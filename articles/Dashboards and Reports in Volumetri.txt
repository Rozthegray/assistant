Dashboards and Reports in Volumetrica
Updated over a week ago

In the Dashboard and Reports section of Volumetrica, you gain access to real-time updates on your trading activity, enabling you to monitor your performance and trading consistency without waiting for end-of-day summaries.

Dashboard
Comprehensive Performance Overview:
The Dashboard presents a holistic view of your account’s performance. It includes critical metrics such as account drawdown and profit goals, providing an immediate snapshot of your trading health.

Pending Orders and Positions:
Pending Orders: This section displays all active pending orders. To manage these orders efficiently, use the “Cancel All” button to remove all pending orders at once.
Positions: The Positions window provides details of your current trades. If you need to quickly close all open positions, click the “Flatten All” button to liquidate them simultaneously.

Reports
Detailed Account Analysis:
The Reports tab offers an in-depth analysis of your trading activity. Key metrics include:
Win Rate: Percentage of trades that were successful.
Largest Win: The highest profit earned from a single trade.
Largest Loss: The greatest loss incurred from a single trade.
Average Loss: The mean loss amount across all trades.
Most Traded Symbols: A list of symbols that you trade most frequently.
Days with Highest Losses: Analysis of weekdays where the most losses occur, helping to identify patterns or trends in your trading performance.
Symbol Performance Statistics: Detailed performance metrics for each symbol traded, allowing you to assess which symbols contribute most to your overall performance.
Average Contract Size: Information on the average size of contracts traded, providing insights into your trading scale and risk exposure.

Utilizing the insights from these reports will aid in refining your trading strategies and enhancing your risk management practices, ultimately leading to improved trading performance.

For any questions or further assistance regarding the Dashboard and Reports, please contact our support team. We are dedicated to helping you optimize your trading experience.
