Trading 101 in Volumetrica
Updated over a week ago

Placing trades on Volumetrica is designed to be intuitive and efficient, offering various trading methods including OCO orders.

Types of Orders:
- Market: Buy or sell at current price.
- Limit: Set a specific price for buy/sell.
- Stop: Trigger trade at a specified price.

How to Place a Trade:
1. Enable trading (toggle button).
2. Select quantity, account.
3. Choose Market/Limit/Stop.
4. Configure OCO (Single/Multi), set brackets.
5. SL/TP can be dragged directly on chart.

Stop/Limit:
- Click LMT Buy/Sell, hover to pick price, left-click to confirm.
- OCO: Toggle OCO for brackets, fill out settings.
- Multi-OCO: add via popup, set configuration, save strategy.

To flatten all, use FLAT button.

Support available via customer service.
