At MyFunded Futures, we understand that a trader's career hinges on their ability to progress without compromise. To support this progression from simulated funding stages to live funding stages, we have introduced the Starter Account at MyFunded Futures.

Main Objective: Transition to Live Funding
Our primary objective is to guide traders toward stable income and promote them to a live funding environment.

The Starter Plan is designed to nurture a mindset focused on steadily growing the account.

Note: In the evaluation stages there aren't any consistency rules. Traders may pass the evaluation in a single trading day.

Benefits of the Starter Account

Easiest Path to Live Funding: Consistent traders can be utilized for proprietary funding methods.
Potential Weekly Payouts: After five winning days, traders can receive payouts, e.g., 5 days of $100 (50K account).
Daily Loss Limits: Ensure you have another day to survive in the markets. Daily Loss Limit does not disqualify your account.
Competitive Pricing: Low barrier of entry with some of the most competitive prices in the market.
No Buffer Zone: No buffer zone in starter plans. Reach 5 profitable days, eligible for payout. Drawdown moves to balance +100 at payout request.

Frequently Asked Questions
Are there consistency rules in evaluation?  
No. The 40% consistency rule applies only to the funded stage and resets after each payout.

Is scaling rule present in evaluation?  
Not present in evaluation, but is present in Sim Funded stages.

Will hitting the Daily Loss Limit fail my account?  
No. It's a soft-breach; stops you for the trading day.

Are there buffer zones in the Starter Account payout policy?  
No. No buffer zone on New Starter Accounts.

Are there activation fees?  
No activation fees.

What's the minimum payout amount?  
$250.

Do I need to be net positive between payout cycles?  
Yes, net positive by at least $500 to request a payout.

Can I trade on live and sim at the same time?  
No, not allowed.
